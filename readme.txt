Customizable array of social share buttons for Wordpress .


== Description ==

Wordpress social share and bookmark buttons is a widget which allows to display , on your wordpress website , a customizable array of social share buttons which give internet surfers the opportunity of recommending the visited website , through well-known social networks .

This widget has the originality to display large buttons that appear and disappear randomly with a jQuery script .

Wordpress social share and bookmark buttons widget ( for facebook , twitter , google plus one , etc ... ) is distinguished by a prefix to avoid conflicts in database Options table .

= [ WIDGET INSTALLATION PROCEDURE ] =


**1. WIDGET INSTALLATION :**

* Download Wordpress social share and bookmark buttons ( for facebook , twitter , google plus one , etc ...  ) to your computer , from official Wordpress directory .
* Launch your web browser and connect you to your Wordpress blog .
* Click on the plugins menu .
* Click on the Add New button .
* Click on the Upload link .
* Use the browse button to select the plugin zip file that was downloaded .
* Click on the Install Now button .
* Once the widget is installed, click on the Activate plugin button .
* The widget is now ready to use !
 

**2. WIDGET CONFIGURATION :**

* You can access Wordpress social share and bookmark buttons ( for facebook , twitter , google plus one , etc ...  ) from  Appearance --> Widget menu or from widgets option of your theme .
* Drag and drop Wordpress social share and bookmark buttons ( for facebook , twitter , google plus one , etc ...  ) towards the appropriate sidebar .
* Enter a title for this widget .

**ATTENTION :**

* Adjust share buttons container height to see all share buttons correctly
* Whether for share buttons container or share buttons , 3 px border width are recommended for a "double" border style .
* Whether for share buttons container or share buttons , leave "background field" empty for a transparent background .
* Facebook Url Page and Facebook App Id are required , if "Facebook" social share button is selected .
* You can manage all other parameters , at your convenience 
* A tutorial explaining how to retrieve a Facebook App Id is available at the following address :

[http://donisocial.donimedia-servicetique.net/?p=49](http://donisocial.donimedia-servicetique.net/?p=49 "How to retrieve a Facebook App Id - DoniSocial tutorial") .

* Once your web page is refreshed , you can see that Wordpress social share and bookmark buttons ( for facebook , twitter , google plus one , etc ...  ) is now ready to use !


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**[ How to retrieve a Facebook App id and a Facebook App secret ? ]**

Facebook App id and Facebook App secret are two parameters required to use Wordpress Social Facebook All In One widget .

More precisely, Facebook app id is a parameter , sometimes called Facebook client id, required to use Facebook applications , including registration box .

Facebook app secret is a number , only known by you and Facebook , which is used to signed your Facebook data .
